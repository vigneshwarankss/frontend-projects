About Nagapattinam

Tamil Nadu's Coastal Treasure

Nagapattinam, a coastal town in Tamil Nadu, is renowned for its religious significance, beautiful beaches, and rich history. A perfect blend of spirituality and natural beauty.

Religious Harmony

Home to sacred sites of Christianity, Islam, and Hinduism coexisting peacefully.

Pristine Beaches

Miles of untouched coastline offering tranquility and breathtaking sunrises.

Historical Legacy

Once a major Chola port, with archaeological remains dating back centuries.

Popular Tourist Places

Explore Nagapattinam's most famous spiritual and historical sites

Christian
15 km from city

Velankanni Church

Open: 5:30 AM - 9:00 PM
Entry: Free

Our Lady of Good Health, a revered Catholic shrine known as the "Lourdes of the East". Pilgrims believe in the miraculous healing powers of this sacred site.

Read More →
Islamic
5 km from city

Nagore Dargah

Open: 4:30 AM - 9:30 PM
Entry: Free

A 16th-century Islamic shrine dedicated to Sufi saint Hazrat Syed Shahul Hameed Quadir. Known for its annual 14-day Kanduri festival attracting devotees from all faiths.

Read More →
Hindu
20 km from city

Sikkal Singaravelan Temple

Open: 6:00 AM - 12:30 PM, 4:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Entry: Free

Famous for Lord Murugan holding a vel (spear), believed to have been worshipped by the Chola kings. The temple comes alive during the Skanda Sashti festival.

Read More →
Hindu
35 km from city

Thirunallar Saneeswaran Temple

Open: 5:30 AM - 12:30 PM, 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Entry: Free

Dedicated to Lord Shani (Saturn), this temple is believed to alleviate the malefic effects of Saturn. Special abhishekams are performed on Saturdays.

Read More →
Hindu
10 km from city

Soundararajaperumal Temple

Open: 6:30 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Entry: Free

A beautiful Vaishnavite temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his Soundararaja form. The temple architecture showcases exquisite Chola craftsmanship.

Coastal Beauty

Pristine Beaches of Nagapattinam

Discover serene coastlines perfect for relaxation and breathtaking sunrises

Velankanni Beach

Adjacent to the famous church, this beach offers spiritual serenity with ocean views.

15 km from Nagapattinam
Best for: Sunrise views, Pilgrimage extension

Kodinagar Beach

Less crowded with golden sands, ideal for peaceful walks and photography.

8 km from Nagapattinam
Best for: Solitude, Bird watching

Poompuhar Beach

Historic beach near the ancient Chola port city, with a maritime museum nearby.

30 km from Nagapattinam
Best for: History enthusiasts, Sunset

Beach Safety Tips

  • Swim only in designated safe zones
  • Beware of strong currents, especially during monsoon
  • Carry drinking water and sun protection
  • Respect local customs (some beaches near temples may have dress codes)

Best Time to Visit

November - February

Pleasant weather (22°C - 30°C), ideal for beach activities

March - June

Hot (28°C - 38°C), early mornings and evenings recommended

July - October

Monsoon season, some beaches may have restricted access
